Our privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") is designed to help you understand how we collect, use and share your personal information and to help you exercise the privacy rights available to you.</p>

<h2>Your data belongs to you</h2>
<p>Your data does not belong to us. Putting notes and other content on African Puzzle works does not change the ownership and copyright status of the content in question. If the data originally belonged to you, it continues to belong to you once you have placed it on African Puzzle works. Naturally, if the data did not originally belong to you, putting it on African Puzzle works does not give you ownership in any way. This applies to all our products and services.</p>
<p>By using our products, you authorize African Puzzle works to perform certain actions with your data in order to operate our service. For example, you authorize us to save it within the time limits of legal regulations, send it over a network, index it so you can search for it later, display it on your different devices, etc. Some of these operations may require us to send your data to our regular business partners (such as our network operator) to whom we have entrusted the execution of part of the African Puzzle works service. We always ensure that our contracts with these partners protect your property rights.</p>
<p>Apart from authorizing us to perform these limited operations to run the African Puzzle works service, you retain all rights relating to your data.</p>

<h2>Scope</h2>
<p>This privacy policy covers the personal information we collect about you when you use our products or services, or interact with us in any other way, including on our website at <a href="https://africanpuzzle.com">www.africanpuzzle.com</a>, our mobile application ("<strong>App</strong>") and our WhatsApp group related online service ("<strong>Service</strong>"). This policy also explains the choices available to you regarding how we use your personal information, including how you can object to certain uses of information and how you can access and update certain information.</p>

<h2>1. Personal information we collect</h2>
<p>We collect personal information when you provide it to us, when you use our services and when other sources provide it to us, as described below.</p>

<h3>A. Information you provide to us</h3>
<p><strong>Account creation:</strong> When you create an account or use the Services, we collect information such as your name, email address, password and your role within your team or company. If you choose to grant permission, the African Puzzle WORKS application accesses your device's contacts and photos to enhance your experience with the application. African Puzzle WORKS does not store your content on company servers, meaning any content you save in the application - such as text, images, photos, contacts, numbers and voice notes - is stored only on your device. We respect your privacy and will never share this information without your explicit consent.</p>
<p><strong>Your communications with us:</strong> We collect personal information such as your email address, phone number or mailing address when you request information about our services, subscribe to our newsletter, request technical or customer support, or otherwise communicate with us. We also collect the content of messages or attachments you may send us, as well as other information you choose to provide, which may be associated with your communications.</p>
<p><strong>Payment information:</strong> When you choose to subscribe to our service, we collect payment information to enable you to pay us. We use third-party payment providers to process payments on the Services. We may receive information associated with your payment information, such as billing address and transaction information, but we do not directly store payment information on the Services.</p>
<p><strong>Surveys:</strong> We may contact you to participate in surveys. If you decide to participate, you may be asked to provide certain information that may include personal data.</p>
<p><strong>Interactive features:</strong> We may offer interactive features such as forums, blogs, chat and messaging services, and social media pages. We and other users of our services may collect information that you submit or make available through these interactive features. Any content you provide through the public sections of these features will be considered "public" and will not be subject to the privacy protections mentioned herein.</p>
<p><strong>Lottery or contest registration:</strong> We may organize lotteries and contests. The contact information you provide may be used to inform you about draws or contests and for other promotional, marketing and commercial purposes.</p>
<p><strong>Conferences, trade shows and other events:</strong> We may attend conferences, trade shows and other events where we collect personal information from people who interact with or express interest in the services.</p>
<p><strong>Job applications:</strong> We may post job openings and opportunities on the services. If you respond to one of these postings by submitting your application, CV and/or cover letter, we will collect and use your information to assess your qualifications.</p>

<h3>B. Information collected automatically</h3>
<p><strong>Automatic data collection:</strong> We keep track of certain information about you when you visit and interact with any of our services. This information may include your Internet Protocol (IP) address, user settings, MAC address, cookie identifiers, mobile carrier, mobile advertising and other unique identifiers, details about your browser, operating system or device, location information (including location inferred from your IP address), your Internet service provider, pages you visit before, during and after using the services, information about links you click, information about how you interact with the services including frequency and duration of your activities, and other information about how you use the services.</p>
<p><strong>Cookies, Pixel Tags/Web Beacons, and Analytics Information:</strong> We, as well as third parties that provide content, advertising or other features on the Services, may use cookies, pixel tags, local storage and other technologies ("Technologies") to automatically collect information through the Services.</p>
<p><strong>Cookies:</strong> Cookies are small text files placed in device browsers to remember their preferences. Most browsers allow you to block and delete cookies. However, if you do so, the services may not function properly.</p>
<p><strong>Pixel Tags / Web Beacons:</strong> A pixel tag (also known as a web beacon) is a piece of code embedded in services that collects information about engagement in services.</p>
<p><strong>Analytics:</strong> We may also use third-party service providers to collect and process analytics data and other information about our services.</p>

<h3>C. Information from other sources</h3>
<p>We may obtain information about you from other sources, including through third-party services and organizations. If you choose to use the Google Contacts feature in our services, we will be able to view your contacts via the Google People API. The sole use of this data is to populate auto-completion of your contacts when sending invitation emails. African Puzzle works will not use this data for any other purpose.</p>
